About me

Who am I?

My name is Petr Kovář. I am 21 years old and I live in Česká Skalice. I study at Technical University of Liberec.

Since I was a child, I have always been very curious and I asked a lot of questions. Even when I could barely speak, I remembered many car brands, and cars are still one of my biggest interests today.

I am also interested in technology and its future. I have always wanted to understand how things work and why they work that way. That is also one of the reasons why I study at TUL.

My interests

Technology, cars and creativity

Cars, modern technology, electronics and practical projects are topics that motivate me. Since I was young, I have been interested in how things work and why they work that way.

I enjoy working with electronics and technical projects where I need to think, solve problems and find a functional solution. I like situations where I can apply logical thinking and create something that actually works in real life.

I also have basic experience with programming, mainly as part of my studies, but I focus more on practical technical work such as electronics and project design.

Overall, I like learning new things, improving my skills and understanding how different systems work, especially in the field of technology and engineering.
